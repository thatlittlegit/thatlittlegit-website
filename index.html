I plan to have GRUB not connected to any distro, on a separate /boot.

As for hardware, I have an Acer Aspire E1-572P, with an Intel Core i5-4200U and Intel HD GRAPHIX! It has 1TB of disk, though, and 12GB RAM, so it's not really that bad.
